History

With this technical know-how, ODOC created its menu communicating and taste-testing with customers.

Philosophy



ODOC's main goal is 'Let people feel REAL yogurt'. Many franchise cafes imitate yogurt's flavor with yogurt powder and milk, not fermenting the yogurt.

ODOC makes authentic yogurt directly in-house with no sugar and no artificial additives.
